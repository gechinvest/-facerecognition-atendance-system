❓ What is KYC & What's Its Purpose?

KYC = Know Your Customer - is the process of verifying a person's identity to ensure they are who they claim to be.

Purposes of KYC in this system:
• 🔒 Security - Prevents unauthorized access and identity fraud
• 👥 Attendance - Verifies employees are physically present for check-in
• 🚪 Door Access - Ensures only authorized personnel can enter restricted areas
• 📊 Compliance - Provides audit trail for security and regulatory purposes
• ✅ Trust - Builds confidence that the system can reliably identify people
